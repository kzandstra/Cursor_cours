The Spiritual Meaning

Many people see sunflowers as a spiritual flower. This is because of their similar appearance to the sun and the fact that they’re always trying to find the light.

Sunflowers symbolise loyalty and following a spiritual guide. Having these bright, sunny flowers around can help reduce feelings of stress and anxiety.

Self-Care Tip

Include sunflowers in teas, bath bombs, and massage oils for the ultimate at-home self-care ritual!

An Unmistakable Palette

Yellow

Goodness, truth and timelessness.

Red

Strength and positivity.

Orange

Energy, happiness and fortune.

White

White sunflowers are probably the rarest of them all. They're not a true white, more of a very pale yellow with a black centre. White sunflowers mean purity, re-birth and peace.

Pink

Innocence and good health.

Purple

Wealth, glory and royalty.
